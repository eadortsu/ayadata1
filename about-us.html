About Us

Discover The Competitive Advantages Of Ghana’s Data Professionals

  • Aya Data was founded on the confidence to create opportunity where talent already exists.
  • We’ve grown in Ghana, where delivering high-quality customer outcomes forms part of the national identity. We even chose “Aya”” in tribute to the traditional Adinkra symbol for resilience and endurance, after experiencing local data experts’ capacity to produce world class tech services through skills acquired both domestically and abroad.
  • After 12 years of building successful enterprises in West Africa, we realised an opportunity to showcase the abilities of resourceful Ghanaian professionals to deliver first rate data services to companies around the world.
  • Our mission at Aya Data is to provide world-class data services whilst supporting Ghana’s youth, enabling them to generate a stable income and build transferrable skills. Despite significant socioeconomic gains, Ghanaians (particularly women) face constant challenges seeking stable employment. The jobs market is far less developed than that of the education system, resulting in graduates taking an average of 7 years to find professional work. Yet the education sector also produces high-calibre, ambitious individuals ready to thrive under the right circumstances.
  • As well as providing our consultants with life-changing training and career opportunities, we give global companies access to flexible and affordable high-quality data labelling and annotation services.
