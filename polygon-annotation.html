POLYGON ANNOTATION

INCREASE AI ACCURACY THROUGH PRECISION OBJECT IDENTIFICATION

  • When precisely defining shapes within an image, polygon annotation allows AI to recognise objects that are otherwise difficult to detect.
  • Polygon annotation surpasses bounding boxes when detecting and segmenting irregular-shaped objects. This is vital in real-world environments, which feature irregular shapes far more than regular ones. Irregularities include multiple unique shapes with greater curves, angles and lines, coarse features such as logos and graphics, and objects from aerial footage. The tool affords annotators greater flexibility in accurately capturing objects for labelling.
  • This particular technique carries a high degree of responsibility to ensure that your AI receives the correct training. We communicate with you throughout our collaborative process to deliver accurately labelled data as part of our service.
